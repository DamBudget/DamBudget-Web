All user data is securely stored in Apple’s iCloud, under your personal account. Apple provides the storage and security infrastructure for your data. Learn more about Apple’s privacy practices here: Apple Privacy Policy.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit any data to third parties. No data ever leaves your device or your iCloud account.

Analytics and Tracking

This app does not use analytics tools, tracking technologies, advertising frameworks, or any third-party SDKs.

Your Rights and Control

Since your data is stored in your personal iCloud account, you can manage, back up, or delete it at any time using your device’s iCloud settings.
